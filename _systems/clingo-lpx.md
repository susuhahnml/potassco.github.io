_clingo-lpx_ is an extension of the _clingo_ ASP system that adds first-class
linear constraints over rational numbers, supporting both strict and non‑strict
relations. Linear constraints are delegated to an embedded simplex solver,
enabling tight integration of constraint reasoning and answer-set solving for
efficient modeling of numerical conditions within ASP.
